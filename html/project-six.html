FreeWay APP

Estimator for Traveling Time

This project was build with Javascript, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDatabase collections, CSV, and CSS.

In the FreeWay App project, me and my team members designed a web application to access the MongoDatabase collections. Use the information provided to estimate the traveling time and the volume of traffic of a station within a selected time interval.
